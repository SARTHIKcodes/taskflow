Task Flow

A free, lightweight Kanban board built for people who want to get things done without complexity.

No sign-up, no subscription, no clutter — just a clean workspace where you can organize your tasks and actually focus on your work.

Features

Kanban Board Layout — Visualize your work across three stages: To Do, In Progress, and Done. See everything at a glance and never lose track of a task again.
Task Priority Levels — Mark every task as Low, Medium, or High priority so you always know what needs your attention first.
Real-time Stats — The header shows you Total Cards, Completed tasks, and Work In Progress count at all times — no digging around needed.
Add & Delete Tasks — Create tasks with a title, description, and priority in seconds. Remove them just as fast when they're done.
Persistent Storage — Your tasks are saved in your browser automatically. Close the tab, restart your computer — your work is still there when you come back.
Clean Dark UI — Designed to be easy on your eyes during long work sessions. No flashy distractions, just your tasks.
Lightweight — Loads instantly, never slows you down
Private — Your tasks never leave your device, no data is sent anywhere
